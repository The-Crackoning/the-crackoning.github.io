Reporting Mechanism

To Report people on The Crackoning you can check out quick-report. This Channel is primarily for quickly reporting someone.

Should you have a problem with a person where there has been conflicts with them for a far longer period (ON THE SERVER) that is not quickly covered by saying "Okay ya broke this rule, so ya get punished" or in a very difficult to judge area, then use in-depth-report.
